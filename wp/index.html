Adding Carousel to WordPress

Go to your WP Plugins page and click "Add New"

Then click "Upload Plugin"

Next, select the "ThetaCarousel.zip" file from the plugin's main download folder.

Then click the "Activate Plugin" button

Now you are ready to create your first Theta Carousel
